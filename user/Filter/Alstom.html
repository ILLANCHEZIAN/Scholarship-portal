About The Program

Alstom India Scholarship 2024-25: Phase-III is an initiative by Alstom India that aims to provide financial support to students across India to help them pursue their higher education and prevent them from dropping out of their colleges. Under this scholarship, students pursuing ITI/Diploma, general graduation, or professional graduation in STEM will be provided with one-time financial assistance of up to INR 75,000 to cover their academic expenses.

Eligibility Deadline:Always Open

  • Students enrolled in any year of general graduation courses such as B. Com, BBA, BA, B.Sc. for FY 2024-25 are eligible.
  • Applicants must have secured at least 60% marks in their previous academic year/semester.
  • The annual family income of the applicants must be less than or equal to INR 6,00,000 from all sources.
  • The scholarship is open to students studying in locations such as Coimbatore (Tamil Nadu), Madhepura (Bihar), Sri City (Andhra Pradesh), or Vadodara (Gujarat) at specified colleges

Note

  • Please click here to check the complete list of specified colleges.
  • Students receiving a scholarship from other sources during the same academic year are not eligible to apply.
  • The scholarship is open for all categories (no preference will be given to students belonging to any of the categories). Selection will be made only based on the given eligibility criteria.
