About The Program

UQ International High Achievers Award - India 2025 is an opportunity offered by the University of Queensland, Australia, for students enrolled in undergraduate and postgraduate courses. The scholarship aims to assist with tuition fees for international students from selected countries pursuing a relevant degree program at the university. The selected students will receive a tuition fee reduction of 20% per annum for the enrolled coursework program

Eligibility Deadline:Always Open

  • be an international student from selected countries including India
  • hold a valid passport
  • received an unconditional offer to undertake studies at the University of Queensland on a full-time basis in a relevant undergraduate or postgraduate program, commencing in 2025
  • have relevant program entry rank
  • fulfil the English language proficiency requirements
  • have achieved a competitive score as determined by the University.

Note

  • Students must not be receiving any other scholarship/tuition fee reduction or rebate from the University of Queensland.
  • Students must not be receiving a full scholarship/sponsorship from another organisation.
